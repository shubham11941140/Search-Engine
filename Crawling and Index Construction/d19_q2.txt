Wild Animals (야생동물 보호구역, Yasaeng dongmul bohoguyeog) is Korean director Kim Ki-duk's second film, released in 1997. [1]It is a crime-drama film set in Paris, and stars Cho Jae-hyun, Dong-jik Jang and Ryun Jang.
A North Korean and a South Korean meet each other by chance in Paris and become involved in the intrigues of the Paris underworld.
